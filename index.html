About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Ellarina & Pacto Café, where every cup tells a story. Established in 2025, our café is dedicated to creating a warm and inviting space where people can connect over their love for coffee and great conversations.

Our Mission

At Ellarina & Pacto Café, we take pride in serving only the finest, ethically sourced coffee beans from around the world, brewed to perfection. From handcrafted espresso to refreshing teas and delicious pastries, we aim to provide an exceptional experience with every visit.
